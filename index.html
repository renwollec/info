What project is about

The world is absorbed in smartphone screens, and we live in these black mirrors that we refuse to let go off. Smartphones are no longer just a way to communicate with others; they are a pocket world in which we exist. Corporations use this to sell their services and products.

Welcome to Renwollec.

Renwollec, is a long-term project based on the Solana blockchain. We observed numerous projects and noticed that no one is monetizing what theoretically could, to be specific, the visual part of NFT. So we decided to create a project with Dynamic Assets where NFT itself represents art with space for integrated advertising that could be changed anytime through an advertisement platform which we developping.
By showing's the partner's advertisement, the holder will receive rewards.
Read more about Benefits & Utilities here below.

Benefits & Utilities

Token project

By showing's the partner's advertisement, the holder will receive a reward - project token.
The project token will be used to:
- convert to SOL
- participation on auctions/raffles/lotteries
- buy NFTs of our partners
and more...

Airdrops

After sold-out will be an airdrop of our token. In that way we could test our platform on beta launch.

Benefits from our partners

Some benefits from partners project like tokens, NFTs, WL or specific exclusive roles on their project and discord server.
The list of our partners will be created on launch of our platform.

Rewarded beta product experience

Beside of advertisement platform will be create other ways to spend tokens and holder will be part of testers.
